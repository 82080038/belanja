## ⚠️ Important Compatibility Notes

### RAM Compatibility
- ✅ **Speed:** 1067MHz required (1333MHz may work but not guaranteed)
- ✅ **Voltage:** Must be 1.5V (1.35V low voltage NOT compatible)
- ✅ **Form Factor:** SODIMM required (DIMM desktop NOT compatible)
- ✅ **Density:** Single-sided or double-sided 4GB modules
- ❌ **DDR4:** Not compatible with DDR3 slots
- ❌ **Different voltages:** May cause system instability

### Battery Compatibility
- ✅ **Model:** AS10D31 or exact equivalent
- ✅ **Voltage:** Must be 11.1V
- ✅ **Connector:** Physical compatibility required
- ✅ **Polarity:** Correct polarity essential
- ❌ **Different models:** May not fit or work
- ❌ **Wrong voltage:** Can damage system

## 🔍 Quality Indicators

### RAM Quality Check
- **Brand Reputation:** Kingston, Crucial, Samsung recommended
- **Warranty:** Lifetime warranty preferred
- **CAS Latency:** Lower CL7 better than CL9
- **Heat Spreaders:** Not required for DDR3 laptop RAM
- **Testing:** Pass MemTest86+ recommended

### Battery Quality Check
- **Capacity:** Higher mAh = longer runtime
- **Brand:** Original Acer or reputable third-party
- **Warranty:** Minimum 6 months
- **Safety Certifications:** CE, RoHS, UN38.3
- **Manufacture Date:** Fresh stock preferred

## 📋 Installation Specifications

### RAM Installation
- **Tools:** Phillips screwdriver, anti-static strap
- **Process:** Bottom panel removal, slot access
- **Orientation:** Notch alignment required
- **Force:** Gentle pressure until clips lock
- **Time:** 5-10 minutes

### Battery Installation
- **Tools:** Phillips screwdriver (may not be needed)
- **Process:** Slide latch, remove old battery
- **Orientation:** Correct connector alignment
- **Force:** Minimal force required
- **Time:** 2-5 minutes
